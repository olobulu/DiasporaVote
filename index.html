THINK LOCAL GO GLOBAL !

#DiasporaVote! ensures greater representation and inclusion for Kenyans living abroad. We empower them to understand their voting rights, advocate for participation, and navigate Kenya's political and electoral processes.

Amplifying the Voices of Kenyans in the Diaspora

#DiasporaVote! ensures greater representation and inclusion for Kenyans living abroad. We help them advocate for their voting rights and stay engaged in Kenya's political and electoral processes.



REPRESENTATION

We act as a bridge for Kenyans in the diaspora to understand how government policies affect their rights and participation.



EDUCATION

We provide insights into Kenya's political and electoral systems, empowering diasporic communities with the knowledge to make informed decisions.



OPPORTUNITIES

Through our programs, we create opportunities for Kenyan youth in the diaspora to engage in policy-making and civic leadership.
