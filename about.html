Who We Are

We are a community of Kenyans living abroad who are united by the belief that our voices should count in shaping Kenya's future.

Our Goals

  • Ensure that Kenyans abroad can vote in national elections.
  • Foster community engagement and advocacy.

Our Vision

A united and active diaspora community influencing positive change in Kenya.

Our Mission

To empower the diaspora to actively participate in Kenya's democratic process.
